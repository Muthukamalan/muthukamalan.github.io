Breath

Muthu Kamalan
29 May 2018

When you give things space to breathe and be as they are without force,
they fall into the right places and serve you the best.
When we try to use our will to make things be a certain way,
we cause more chaos than peace.
Trust that things will fall into the perfect place and do things honestly.
